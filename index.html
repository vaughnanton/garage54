About G54

True automotive passion. Perfection Redefined.

As long as there have been cars, there have been mechanics, but at Garage 54, we see cars differently than most shops. We are car people, so we are no nonsense. Instead, we believe in telling it like it is and treating every car as if it is one of our very own passion projects.

While we specialize in European vehicles, we have experience working with every kind of vehicle, domestic or foreign, and every type of service. From oil changes and tune ups, to large-scale maintenance and repairs, to forced induction installs, modifications, alignments, and everything in between.

We are all about tailoring your car to you. Our deep love of cars, coupled with 15+ years of experience, means that every time one of our experts picks up a wrench, you can be assured that your car is in excellent hands. Contact us today!
